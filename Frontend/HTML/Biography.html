Owner's Biography

Kyriq Cole is the owner of Kyriq Cafe and has run the resturaunt for over 30 years.
Over the years the resturaunt has become famous for its shrimp dishes and Kyriq is committed to serving all his customers.
Kyriq started his career in cuisine at a Texas Roadhouse when he was only 16 working his way up from bus boy to owner.
He trained and mastered his cooking techniques until he was well versed enough to open a second resturaunt.
In 2016 he sold all his resturaunts for $100 million. He now spends his time giving back to students at the campus.
In his free time Kyriq loves to grill steak and shrimp. In 2020 Kyriq had a 1 versus 1 with Gordon Ramsay and lost 45-0.
